## Background

I made a decision at some point to only emulate games that I have legitimate access to (see my Piracy policy for more information on that). I own physical copies of Pokemon Yellow, Gold and Ruby and have covered those games on the channel.

## Verdict: Real

I read through [this guide](https://gameverifying.com/wiki/cart-based-systems/nds) and examined my DS cart. Based on images of verified Pokemon Diamond (FRA) carts online and by cross-referencing the codes, I concluded that the cart I now have in my possession is a legitimate copy. Here's why:

3. Label centred

1. PCB Batch Identifier
"DA"
The chart on gameverifying.com notes that "DA" is not known to be used by fake carts.

2. Inked Code on the back of the cart
NTR-005 should have 9 digits, this cart does, matches the front of the cart
ADAFN5J08, if correct, matches verified legit FRA carts

4. PCB Variation, C03-10, matches verified legit FRA carts
